Exploring Mars

Mars was a hotspot in 2021, with three missions from separate countries arriving on the red planet early in the year, and the interest in the fourth planet from the sun is only heating up. prepare for inspiring new flights by NASA'S ingenuity helicopter, which is still going strong beyond its expected life span, and the beginning of the preseverance rover's investigation of the intriguing remains of an ancient river on Mars beginning in the summer.
