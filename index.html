What makes a good vision and mission statement?

A good vision and mission statement is concise and inspirational. It’s easy to memorize and repeat. It should be clear, engaging, and realistic, and describe a bright future. It should state your intentions, summarize your values, and demonstrate your commitment to living up to these values.
